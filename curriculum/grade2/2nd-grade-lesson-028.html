📜 More and More Ingredients (Part 1)

The delicious smell of the soup brought more villagers to the town square. Everyone wanted to see this amazing stone soup being made. The traveler stirred and tasted again.

"This soup is very good," he said thoughtfully. "But it would be even more delicious with some cabbage and beans." More villagers hurried home to get vegetables.

📜 More and More Ingredients (Part 2)

Soon people were bringing all kinds of good things. Someone brought meat. Another person brought herbs and spices. One woman brought fresh bread to go with the soup.

The villagers were getting excited. They had not had a community meal like this in a very long time. Everyone was laughing and talking as they waited for the soup to finish cooking.

📰 Giraffes

Giraffes are the tallest animals in the world, with long necks that can be up to 6 feet long! They live in Africa and use their height to eat leaves from tall acacia trees that other animals cannot reach. Giraffes have long, dark tongues that can be 18 inches long, which helps them grab leaves and branches. Their tall height also helps them see predators like lions from far away. Even though giraffes are very tall, they are gentle animals that live in groups called herds. Baby giraffes are called calves, and they are already 6 feet tall when they are born! Giraffes have unique spotted patterns on their fur, and each giraffe's spots are different, just like human fingerprints. They can run up to 35 miles per hour when they need to escape from danger.
