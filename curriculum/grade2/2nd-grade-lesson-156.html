📜 Trapped Under the Basket (Part 1)

In their panic to hide, Peter and Benjamin made a terrible mistake. They crawled under a large wicker basket that was sitting in the garden, thinking it would provide good shelter. Unfortunately, they had chosen the worst possible hiding spot in the entire garden.

When Mr. McGregor entered his garden, he immediately noticed that his vegetables had been disturbed. Someone had been nibbling his plants! He looked around suspiciously and spotted the basket. Something was definitely moving underneath it, and he could hear small, frightened voices whispering.

📜 Trapped Under the Basket (Part 2)

Without hesitation, Mr. McGregor sat down heavily on top of the basket, trapping the two little rabbits completely. Peter and Benjamin were now prisoners, stuck in the dark underneath the wicker basket with no way to escape. They could hardly breathe in the cramped space.

"I know you're under there, you mischievous rabbits!" chuckled Mr. McGregor. "You won't be stealing any more of my vegetables today!" The two cousins trembled with fear as they realized they were completely at the mercy of the angry gardener. What would happen to them now?
