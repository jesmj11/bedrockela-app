📜 A Desperate Council Meeting (Part 1)

As the situation grew more desperate with each passing day, the wisest and most experienced mice in the community decided that something drastic had to be done. They called for an emergency council meeting, sending secret messages through their network of tunnels and hidden passages to gather all the mice together in the deepest, most secure part of their underground territory.

Mice came from every corner of the house to attend this urgent gathering - young mice who were learning to fend for themselves, middle-aged mice with families to protect, and elderly mice whose long experience had taught them to recognize truly serious threats. They assembled in a large chamber behind the kitchen wall, speaking in hushed whispers about the terrible danger that now controlled their home.

📜 A Desperate Council Meeting (Part 2)

The eldest mouse, whose gray whiskers and careful movements showed his many years of survival experience, called the meeting to order. "My friends," he said solemnly, "we face the greatest threat our community has ever encountered. This cat is not like the lazy house cats we've heard about in other homes. She is a dedicated hunter who will not rest until she has made our lives impossible."

Many mice shared stories of narrow escapes and dangerous encounters with the cat. Some had lost friends and family members to her quick reflexes and sharp claws. Others reported that entire sections of the house were now completely off-limits because the cat had learned to patrol them so thoroughly. The mood in the chamber grew more grim as each mouse described how the cat had disrupted their normal routines and threatened their survival.
