📜 Jack Loves to Jump (Part 1)

There once was a boy named Jack who loved to jump over things. He was very quick and nimble. Every day, Jack would practice jumping over logs, rocks, and puddles.

Jack lived in a small village where people used candles for light at night. In those days, there were no electric lights. Tall candlesticks with bright flames lit up the houses.

📜 Jack Loves to Jump (Part 2)

All the children in the village knew about Jack's jumping skills. They would gather around to watch him practice. "Jump over this!" they would call out, pointing to different objects.

But Jack wanted a bigger challenge. He had jumped over everything in the village except for one thing. The tall candlestick in the town square was his biggest dream to jump over.

📰 Healthy Foods

Healthy foods give our bodies the nutrients they need to grow strong and stay well. Fruits and vegetables provide vitamins and minerals that help our immune system fight off sickness. Whole grains like brown rice and oatmeal give us energy that lasts a long time. Proteins from foods like fish, chicken, beans, and nuts help build and repair our muscles. Eating a variety of healthy foods is important because different foods provide different nutrients that our bodies need. Dairy products like milk and cheese give us calcium for strong bones and teeth. Drinking plenty of water helps our bodies work properly and keeps us hydrated. When we eat healthy foods regularly and limit sugary snacks and drinks, we feel more energetic and are better able to learn and play.
