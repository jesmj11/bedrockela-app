📖 Vocabulary

These words appear in today's chapter. Look for them as you read!

1. courage

Definition: The ability to do something even when you are afraid. Being brave when it is hard.

In the story: "The Lion said he had no courage, yet he leaped out of the trees to frighten the travelers."

2. contradiction

Definition: When two things are opposite or do not match.

In the story: "The Lion is a contradiction: he calls himself a coward, but his actions show bravery."
