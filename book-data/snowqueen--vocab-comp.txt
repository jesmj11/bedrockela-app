The Snow Queen
Chapter Vocabulary & Comprehension Questions
3rd Grade Teacher's Guide

Chapter 1: The Magic Mirror
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. wicked - very bad or evil
	•	2. fragments - small broken pieces
	•	3. shattered - broke into many pieces
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Who created the magic mirror?
	•	2. What terrible power did the mirror have?
	•	3. What happened when the troll tried to take the mirror to heaven?
	•	4. What happened to the mirror when it hit the ground?
	•	5. Why were the tiny pieces more dangerous than the whole mirror?

Chapter 2: Best Friends
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. clever - smart and quick to learn
	•	2. gentle - kind and soft in manner
	•	3. shared - used or enjoyed together
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What were the names of the two best friends?
	•	2. Where did Kay and Gerda like to play together?
	•	3. How were Kay and Gerda different in personality?
	•	4. What stories did Kay's grandmother tell them?
	•	5. Why weren't the children afraid of the Snow Queen?

Chapter 3: The Broken Mirror
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. formed - made or shaped
	•	2. struck - hit suddenly
	•	3. pierced - went through like a sharp object
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What were Kay and Gerda doing when the accident happened?
	•	2. What did Kay think the snowflakes looked like at first?
	•	3. What happened to Kay while they were watching the snow?
	•	4. How did Kay react when Gerda tried to help him?
	•	5. How did the mirror pieces change what Kay could see?

Chapter 4: Kay Changes
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. cruel - mean and unkind
	•	2. disgust - strong dislike
	•	3. wilted - dried up and dying
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How was Kay different after the mirror pieces got inside him?
	•	2. What did Kay say about the roses that he used to love?
	•	3. How did Kay treat Gerda after he changed?
	•	4. What did Kay prefer to do instead of playing with Gerda?
	•	5. How did Gerda feel watching Kay play roughly with other children?

Chapter 5: The Snow Queen's Visit
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. elegant - graceful and stylish
	•	2. magnificent - very impressive and beautiful
	•	3. tinkling - making light, musical sounds
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What appeared in the town square while Kay was playing?
	•	2. What did the Snow Queen look like?
	•	3. What happened when Kay tied his sled to the Snow Queen's sleigh?
	•	4. Why couldn't Kay untie his sled and escape?
	•	5. What happened when the Snow Queen kissed Kay on the forehead?

Chapter 6: Gerda's Sorrow
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. hoarse - rough and harsh sounding
	•	2. refused - would not do something
	•	3. determined - firmly decided
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did everyone do when Kay didn't come home?
	•	2. How long did Gerda search for Kay?
	•	3. What did other people think happened to Kay?
	•	4. Why did Gerda refuse to give up hope?
	•	5. What did Gerda decide to do one spring morning?

Chapter 7: The Journey Begins
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. current - the flow of water in a river
	•	2. downstream - in the direction the river flows
	•	3. climbing - growing up the sides of something
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did Gerda offer to give the river?
	•	2. How did the river respond to Gerda's request?
	•	3. Where did the boat carry Gerda?
	•	4. Who lived in the cottage covered with roses?
	•	5. Why couldn't Gerda stay in the beautiful garden?

Chapter 8: The Crow's Help
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. wise - having great knowledge and good judgment
	•	2. secrets - things that are hidden or not known by everyone
	•	3. sympathy - feeling sorry for someone's troubles
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Who did Gerda meet that offered to help her?
	•	2. What news did the crow share about a nearby kingdom?
	•	3. Why did the crow think the prince might be Kay?
	•	4. How did the crow help Gerda get into the palace?
	•	5. What did Gerda discover when she saw the prince?

Chapter 9: The Robber Girl
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. carriage - a vehicle pulled by horses
	•	2. attacked - suddenly tried to hurt or harm
	•	3. fierce - wild and aggressive
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What gifts did the kind princess give to Gerda?
	•	2. What happened to Gerda as she traveled through the dark forest?
	•	3. How was the robber girl different from other people Gerda had met?
	•	4. What did the robber girl show Gerda?
	•	5. How did the robber girl decide to help Gerda?

Chapter 10: The Reindeer's Gift
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. faithful - loyal and trustworthy
	•	2. blizzards - severe snowstorms
	•	3. crystals - clear pieces that look like glass or ice
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Where did the reindeer carry Gerda?
	•	2. What did the land of the Snow Queen look like?
	•	3. Why couldn't the reindeer take Gerda all the way to the palace?
	•	4. What happened when the snow soldiers surrounded Gerda?
	•	5. What protected Gerda from the icy soldiers?

Chapter 11: The Ice Palace
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. glittering - shining with many small lights
	•	2. throne - a special chair for a king or queen
	•	3. expressionless - showing no emotion on the face
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What was the Snow Queen's palace made of?
	•	2. Where did Gerda find Kay in the palace?
	•	3. How did Kay look different from when Gerda remembered him?
	•	4. What game was Kay trying to play with pieces of ice?
	•	5. Why couldn't Kay hear Gerda when she called to him?

Chapter 12: Love Melts Ice
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. melted - changed from solid to liquid because of heat
	•	2. recognizing - knowing who someone is
	•	3. eternity - forever; time without end
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What happened when Gerda's tears fell on Kay's chest?
	•	2. How did Kay begin to remember his past?
	•	3. What happened when Kay started crying?
	•	4. How did Kay react when he could see clearly again?
	•	5. What important lesson did Kay and Gerda learn about friendship?

Discussion Questions for the Complete Story
	•	1. How did the magic mirror pieces change Kay's personality?
	•	2. What character traits did Gerda show throughout her journey?
	•	3. How did different people help Gerda on her quest to find Kay?
	•	4. What does this story teach us about the power of love and friendship?
	•	5. Why was Gerda able to break the Snow Queen's spell when no one else could?
	•	6. How did both Kay and Gerda change during their adventure?
	•	7. What does the story teach us about not giving up on people we love?
	•	8. How did the evil magic mirror cause problems, and how was it overcome?

Writing Prompts
	•	1. Write about a time when you didn't give up on helping a friend, even when it was difficult.
	•	2. Describe what you think happened to Kay and Gerda in the years after they returned home.
	•	3. Write a letter from Gerda to one of the people who helped her, thanking them for their kindness.
	•	4. Create a new adventure where Kay and Gerda help someone else who is in trouble.
	•	5. Write about what you think makes a friendship strong enough to overcome any obstacle.
