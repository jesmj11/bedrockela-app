The Twelve Dancing Princesses
Chapter Vocabulary & Comprehension Questions
3rd Grade Teacher's Guide

Chapter 1: The Mysterious Secret
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. arranged - placed in a neat, organized way
	•	2. securely - safely and tightly
	•	3. tattered - torn and ragged
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How many daughters did the king have?
	•	2. How were the princesses' beds arranged in their room?
	•	3. What did the king do every evening before the princesses went to sleep?
	•	4. What strange thing did the servants discover every morning?
	•	5. What made this mystery so puzzling?

Chapter 2: The King's Worry
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. troubled - worried and upset
	•	2. expense - the cost of something
	•	3. mysteriously - in a puzzling way that's hard to explain
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Why was the king troubled by the mystery?
	•	2. What worried the king more than the cost of new shoes?
	•	3. How did the princesses respond when their father asked where they went?
	•	4. What did the king try to do to solve the mystery?
	•	5. What happened to the guards the king posted?

Chapter 3: The Royal Challenge
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. proclamation - an official announcement
	•	2. magnificent - very impressive and beautiful
	•	3. banished - forced to leave and never return
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did the king decide to do to solve the mystery?
	•	2. What reward did the king offer for solving the puzzle?
	•	3. How long did each person have to discover the secret?
	•	4. What would happen to someone who failed?
	•	5. Why were young princes eager to accept the challenge?

Chapter 4: Failed Attempts
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. confidence - belief in your ability to succeed
	•	2. boasted - bragged about what he could do
	•	3. potion - a liquid with magical or medicinal powers
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did the first prince feel about his chances of success?
	•	2. Where did the king put the prince so he could watch?
	•	3. What did the eldest princess bring to the prince?
	•	4. What was secretly in the wine?
	•	5. Why did all the other young men fail in the same way?

Chapter 5: The Soldier's Arrival
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. wounded - hurt or injured
	•	2. observant - good at noticing things
	•	3. military - related to the army or soldiers
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Who heard about the challenge after many princes had failed?
	•	2. How was the soldier different from the princes who had tried before?
	•	3. Why did the soldier think he had nothing to lose?
	•	4. Who did the soldier meet on his way to the palace?
	•	5. What kind act did the soldier do for the old woman?

Chapter 6: The Old Woman's Gift
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. shimmered - shone with a soft, flickering light
	•	2. invisible - unable to be seen
	•	3. compelled - forced to do something
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What magical gift did the old woman give to the soldier?
	•	2. What special power did the magic cloak have?
	•	3. Why did the old woman decide to help the soldier?
	•	4. What important warning did she give him?
	•	5. How did the old woman know about the drugged wine?

Chapter 7: The Magic Cloak
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. humbly - in a modest way, not proudly
	•	2. predicted - said what would happen before it happened
	•	3. pretended - acted like something was true when it wasn't
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did the king feel when the soldier arrived?
	•	2. What did the eldest princess offer the soldier?
	•	3. How did the soldier avoid drinking the drugged wine?
	•	4. What did the soldier do to make the princesses think he was asleep?
	•	5. What happened when the soldier put on the magic cloak?

Chapter 8: Following the Secret
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. descended - went down
	•	2. winding - twisting and turning
	•	3. nervously - with worry and fear
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did the eldest princess open the secret passage?
	•	2. What did the princesses do before going down the stairs?
	•	3. Which princess felt like someone was following them?
	•	4. How did the oldest princess respond to her sister's concerns?
	•	5. What did the underground passage look like?

Chapter 9: The Underground Kingdom
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. magnificent - extremely beautiful and impressive
	•	2. chimed - made musical bell-like sounds
	•	3. reflected - bounced light back like a mirror
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Describe the three different forests they walked through.
	•	2. What did the soldier take from each forest?
	•	3. Why did the youngest princess keep hearing sounds?
	•	4. How did the other princesses explain the noises?
	•	5. What did the soldier plan to use the branches for?

Chapter 10: The Dancing Palace
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. crystal - clear, glass-like material
	•	2. compelled - forced to do something by magic or strong feeling
	•	3. twinkled - sparkled with small lights
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Who was waiting by the lake for the princesses?
	•	2. What was special about the twelve princes?
	•	3. How did the soldier get across the lake?
	•	4. Why did the youngest princess's boat feel heavier?
	•	5. What did the princesses do in the crystal palace?

Chapter 11: Gathering Evidence
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. evidence - proof that something happened
	•	2. dawn - the first light of morning
	•	3. summoned - called to come
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Why did the princesses have to return home at dawn?
	•	2. What did the soldier take from the crystal palace?
	•	3. How did the princesses hide their secret when they returned?
	•	4. Where did the soldier hide his evidence?
	•	5. How many nights did the soldier follow the princesses?

Chapter 12: The Truth Revealed
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. confessed - admitted the truth about something
	•	2. enchanted - under a magic spell
	•	3. enchantment - a magic spell
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did the king feel when he called the soldier to the throne room?
	•	2. What proof did the soldier show to the king?
	•	3. How did the princesses react when the soldier revealed their secret?
	•	4. What reward did the king give to the soldier?
	•	5. How did the story end for everyone in the kingdom?

Discussion Questions for the Complete Story
	•	1. Why do you think the princesses kept their dancing a secret?
	•	2. How did the soldier's kindness to the old woman help him succeed?
	•	3. What qualities did the soldier have that the princes lacked?
	•	4. Why was the youngest princess more cautious than her sisters?
	•	5. What does this story teach us about the importance of telling the truth?
	•	6. How did the soldier show cleverness throughout his adventure?
	•	7. Why was it important for the soldier to gather evidence before revealing the secret?
	•	8. What does this story teach us about being observant and patient?

Writing Prompts
	•	1. Write about a time when you discovered something that others had kept secret.
	•	2. Describe what you think the underground kingdom looked like in detail.
	•	3. Write a letter from one of the princesses to a friend, explaining why they kept their dancing secret.
	•	4. Create a new adventure for the soldier using his magic cloak.
	•	5. Write about a time when being patient and observant helped you solve a problem.
