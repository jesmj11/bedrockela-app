Rapunzel
Chapter Vocabulary & Comprehension Questions
3rd Grade Teacher's Guide

Chapter 1: The Longing
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. longing - a strong feeling of wanting something badly
	•	2. trespassed - entered someone's property without permission
	•	3. craving - a powerful desire for something
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What had the man and woman wanted for many years?
	•	2. Whose garden could they see from their window?
	•	3. Why was everyone afraid of entering the garden?
	•	4. What exciting news did the woman discover?
	•	5. What plant in the garden did she begin to crave?

Chapter 2: The Enchanted Garden
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. wasting - becoming thin and weak
	•	2. terrified - very frightened
	•	3. eagerly - with excitement and enthusiasm
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What was happening to the woman because of her craving?
	•	2. Why was the husband willing to enter the witch's garden?
	•	3. When did he decide to go into the garden?
	•	4. How did his wife react when he brought her the rapunzel?
	•	5. What gave the husband courage to risk going into the garden?

Chapter 3: Caught by the Witch
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. pleaded - begged earnestly
	•	2. shrieked - screamed loudly
	•	3. stammered - spoke with difficulty due to fear
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Why did the woman want her husband to get more rapunzel?
	•	2. What happened when the man entered the garden the second time?
	•	3. How did the man try to explain his actions to the witch?
	•	4. What question did the witch ask about the man's wife?
	•	5. What did the man promise to do to protect his family?

Chapter 4: The Terrible Bargain
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. mercy - kindness toward someone you have power to punish
	•	2. condition - a requirement that must be met
	•	3. punishment - something bad done to someone who has broken rules
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did the witch offer to give the man?
	•	2. What was the witch's condition for her mercy?
	•	3. How did the man react to the witch's demand?
	•	4. What difficult choice did the witch give the man?
	•	5. Why did the man finally agree to the witch's bargain?

Chapter 5: Life in the Tower
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. isolated - separated from others; alone
	•	2. incredible - amazing; hard to believe
	•	3. seasons - spring, summer, fall, and winter
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did the witch name the baby and why?
	•	2. Describe the tower where Rapunzel lived.
	•	3. How did the witch visit Rapunzel every day?
	•	4. What did Rapunzel not know about the world?
	•	5. How did Rapunzel spend her time in the tower?

Chapter 6: The Golden Hair
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. braid - hair woven together in a long rope
	•	2. melodies - musical tunes or songs
	•	3. meadows - grassy fields
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How long was Rapunzel's hair when she turned sixteen?
	•	2. What did Rapunzel love to do at her window?
	•	3. What creatures came to listen to her singing?
	•	4. What questions did Rapunzel wonder about?
	•	5. What did Rapunzel dream about sometimes?

Chapter 7: A Voice in the Forest
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. pure - clear and beautiful; without flaws
	•	2. approached - came closer to
	•	3. copying - doing the same as someone else
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did the prince hear while riding through the forest?
	•	2. How did the prince feel when he saw Rapunzel?
	•	3. Why couldn't the prince find a way into the tower?
	•	4. What did the prince discover when he hid and watched?
	•	5. How did the prince learn to get into the tower?

Chapter 8: The Prince's Discovery
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. amazement - great surprise
	•	2. nervousness - feeling worried or anxious
	•	3. kingdom - a country ruled by a king or queen
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did Rapunzel react when she first saw the prince?
	•	2. What did the prince tell Rapunzel about himself?
	•	3. What had Dame Gothel told Rapunzel about other people?
	•	4. What did Rapunzel not know about the world?
	•	5. How did the prince feel when he climbed up to the tower?

Chapter 9: Secret Meetings
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. villages - small towns
	•	2. festivals - happy celebrations with music and dancing
	•	3. protection - keeping someone safe from harm
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What wonderful things did the prince tell Rapunzel about?
	•	2. How was the prince's description different from what Dame Gothel had said?
	•	3. What did the prince offer to do for Rapunzel?
	•	4. What question did the prince ask about the tower?
	•	5. How did Rapunzel feel when she spent time with the prince?

Chapter 10: The Witch's Fury
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. fury - extreme anger
	•	2. ungrateful - not thankful for help or kindness
	•	3. transport - to move from one place to another
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What mistake did Rapunzel make when talking to Dame Gothel?
	•	2. How did Dame Gothel react when she learned about the prince?
	•	3. What did the witch do to Rapunzel's hair?
	•	4. Where did Dame Gothel take Rapunzel after cutting her hair?
	•	5. Why did Dame Gothel think cutting the hair would stop the prince?

Chapter 11: Lost and Found
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. despair - feeling hopeless and very sad
	•	2. survived - stayed alive in difficult conditions
	•	3. oases - places in the desert where there is water
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What happened when the prince climbed up to the tower?
	•	2. How did the prince become blind?
	•	3. How did the prince survive in the forest?
	•	4. How did Rapunzel survive in the desert?
	•	5. How did the prince find Rapunzel again?

Chapter 12: Love's True Power
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. restored - brought back; made good again
	•	2. reunited - brought back together after being apart
	•	3. wisdom - good judgment and knowledge
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did Rapunzel react when she found the prince again?
	•	2. What magical thing happened when Rapunzel's tears touched his eyes?
	•	3. What did the prince say had healed his lonely heart?
	•	4. Who was invited to the prince and Rapunzel's wedding?
	•	5. How did the prince and Rapunzel rule their kingdom?

Discussion Questions for the Complete Story
	•	1. How did the father's choice to steal the rapunzel affect the whole family?
	•	2. Why do you think Dame Gothel wanted to keep Rapunzel isolated from the world?
	•	3. What does this story teach us about the importance of freedom?
	•	4. How did Rapunzel show courage throughout her difficult experiences?
	•	5. What role did music and singing play in bringing Rapunzel and the prince together?
	•	6. How did love help both Rapunzel and the prince overcome their challenges?
	•	7. Why was it important that Rapunzel's parents were forgiven and invited to the wedding?
	•	8. What does this story teach us about the power of hope and perseverance?

Writing Prompts
	•	1. Write about a time when you felt trapped or lonely. How did you get through it?
	•	2. Describe what you think Rapunzel's first day in the prince's kingdom was like.
	•	3. Write a letter from Rapunzel to her parents, telling them about her life in the tower.
	•	4. Create a new ending where Rapunzel escapes from the tower on her own.
	•	5. Write about someone you know who shows courage in difficult situations.
