Cinderella
Chapter Vocabulary & Comprehension Questions
3rd Grade Teacher's Guide

Chapter 1: A Loving Mother
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. gentle - kind and soft in manner
	•	2. courage - being brave when things are difficult
	•	3. grief - deep sadness, especially when someone dies
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did Cinderella's mother teach her to always be?
	•	2. What happened to Cinderella's mother?
	•	3. What promise did Cinderella make to her mother?
	•	4. How did Cinderella and her father feel after the mother died?
	•	5. What advice did the mother give that would help Cinderella throughout the story?

Chapter 2: The New Family
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. remarry - to marry again after your first wife or husband dies
	•	2. vain - too proud of how you look
	•	3. appearances - how things look on the outside
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Why did Cinderella's father decide to remarry?
	•	2. What were the stepmother and stepsisters really like?
	•	3. How did they act when the father was around?
	•	4. What mean things did the stepsisters say about Cinderella?
	•	5. How did Cinderella try to treat her new family?

Chapter 3: A Servant in Her Own Home
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. mockingly - in a way that makes fun of someone
	•	2. cinders - small pieces of partly burned coal or wood
	•	3. complained - said that something was wrong or unfair
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What happened to Cinderella's father?
	•	2. How did the stepfamily treat Cinderella after he died?
	•	3. Why did they call her 'Cinderella'?
	•	4. Where did Cinderella have to sleep?
	•	5. How did Cinderella remain strong during these hard times?

Chapter 4: The Magic Tree
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. churchyard - the area of land around a church
	•	2. hazel - a type of tree with small nuts
	•	3. spirit - the part of a person that lives on after they die
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did Cinderella do every day at her mother's grave?
	•	2. What had Cinderella planted there?
	•	3. Who came to rest in the tree's branches?
	•	4. What magical thing happened one day?
	•	5. What did the birds' song seem to promise Cinderella?

Chapter 5: The Royal Invitation
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. messenger - someone who delivers messages
	•	2. curtsies - polite bows that girls and women make
	•	3. velvet - a soft, thick fabric
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What exciting news did the royal messenger bring?
	•	2. How long would the ball last?
	•	3. How did the stepsisters react to the invitation?
	•	4. How did Cinderella help them prepare?
	•	5. What did Cinderella ask, and how did the stepsisters respond?

Chapter 6: Left Behind
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. ridiculous - very silly or unreasonable
	•	2. corsets - tight undergarments that shape the waist
	•	3. embers - small glowing pieces of coal or wood in a dying fire
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What cruel reasons did the stepsisters give for why Cinderella couldn't go?
	•	2. What excuse did the stepmother give?
	•	3. How did Cinderella help her stepfamily get ready?
	•	4. What did Cinderella do after they left?
	•	5. What did Cinderella wish for as she sat by the fire?

Chapter 7: The Fairy Godmother
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. shimmer - to shine with a soft, flickering light
	•	2. transformed - completely changed into something else
	•	3. magnificent - very beautiful and impressive
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Who appeared to Cinderella as she cried by the fireplace?
	•	2. Why did the fairy godmother say she had come to help?
	•	3. What did the fairy godmother transform the pumpkin into?
	•	4. What animals became the horses and carriage drivers?
	•	5. What was special about the carriage that was created?

Chapter 8: The Royal Ball
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. starlight - light that comes from stars
	•	2. graceful - moving in a smooth, beautiful way
	•	3. mysterious - hard to understand or explain
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did Cinderella's dress look like after the transformation?
	•	2. What important warning did the fairy godmother give?
	•	3. How did people react when Cinderella arrived at the palace?
	•	4. Who asked Cinderella to dance first?
	•	5. How did the stepsisters feel about the mysterious princess?

Chapter 9: Midnight's Magic
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. chime - the sound a clock makes when telling time
	•	2. escape - to get away quickly
	•	3. remind - to help someone remember something
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did Cinderella forget while dancing with the prince?
	•	2. What sound reminded her of the fairy godmother's warning?
	•	3. What did Cinderella lose as she ran down the palace steps?
	•	4. What happened to everything at the stroke of midnight?
	•	5. What did Cinderella have left to prove the night was real?

Chapter 10: The Glass Slipper
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. declared - announced something important
	•	2. cushion - a soft pad for sitting or resting on
	•	3. eagerly - with excitement and enthusiasm
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did the prince declare he would do?
	•	2. How did the stepsisters react to this news?
	•	3. What did each stepsister believe about the glass slipper?
	•	4. What did Cinderella do while she listened to them argue?
	•	5. How did the prince arrive at their house?

Chapter 11: The Perfect Fit
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. squeeze - to push or press tightly
	•	2. nevertheless - even so; despite that
	•	3. reluctantly - not wanting to do something
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What happened when the first stepsister tried on the slipper?
	•	2. Why couldn't the second stepsister wear the glass slipper?
	•	3. What did the stepmother say about Cinderella?
	•	4. Why did the prince insist on trying the slipper on Cinderella?
	•	5. What proved that Cinderella was the mysterious princess?

Chapter 12: A Royal Wedding
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. joyfully - with great happiness
	•	2. elegant - graceful and stylish
	•	3. wisdom - knowledge and good judgment
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did the prince react when he realized Cinderella was his princess?
	•	2. How did the stepfamily feel when they learned the truth?
	•	3. How did Cinderella's character stay the same even as a princess?
	•	4. What happened on Cinderella's wedding day?
	•	5. How did Cinderella and the prince rule their kingdom?

Discussion Questions for the Complete Story
	•	1. How did Cinderella's kindness help her throughout the story?
	•	2. What was the most important lesson Cinderella learned from her mother?
	•	3. Why do you think the fairy godmother chose to help Cinderella?
	•	4. How were Cinderella and her stepsisters different in their characters?
	•	5. What does this story teach us about treating others with kindness?
	•	6. Why was it important that Cinderella forgave her stepfamily in the end?
	•	7. What qualities made Cinderella a good choice to be a princess?
	•	8. How did Cinderella's difficult experiences help prepare her to be a good ruler?

Writing Prompts
	•	1. Write about a time when you had to be brave and kind in a difficult situation.
	•	2. If you were Cinderella's fairy godmother, what other magical gifts would you give her?
	•	3. Write a letter from Cinderella to her mother, telling her about the royal wedding.
	•	4. Describe what you think happened to Cinderella and the prince after they got married.
	•	5. Write about someone in your life who shows kindness like Cinderella did.
