Jack and the Beanstalk
Chapter Vocabulary & Comprehension Questions
3rd Grade Teacher's Guide

Chapter 1: Hard Times
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. widow - a woman whose husband has died
	•	2. struggled - worked hard to do something difficult
	•	3. necessities - things that are needed to live
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Who lived in the small cottage?
	•	2. What was Jack's problem with working?
	•	3. What was their only valuable possession?
	•	4. What happened to Milky White one morning?
	•	5. What did Jack's mother decide they had to do?

Chapter 2: Selling the Cow
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. solemnly - seriously and thoughtfully
	•	2. tattered - old and torn
	•	3. mysterious - strange and hard to explain
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How much money did Jack's mother want for the cow?
	•	2. How did Jack feel about selling Milky White?
	•	3. Who did Jack meet on the way to market?
	•	4. What did the old man look like?
	•	5. What question did the old man ask Jack?

Chapter 3: The Magic Beans
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. shimmer - shine with a soft, flickering light
	•	2. pulse - beat like a heart
	•	3. enchanted - fascinated by magic
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did the old man offer Jack instead of gold?
	•	2. How did the magic beans look different from ordinary beans?
	•	3. What did the old man say would happen if Jack planted the beans?
	•	4. Why did Jack decide to make the trade?
	•	5. What mistake did Jack realize he might have made?

Chapter 4: Mother's Anger
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. eagerly - with excitement and enthusiasm
	•	2. disbelief - not able to believe something
	•	3. despair - feeling hopeless and very sad
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did Jack feel when he ran home with the beans?
	•	2. What was Jack's mother expecting him to bring home?
	•	3. How did Jack's mother react when she saw the beans?
	•	4. What did she do with the magic beans in her anger?
	•	5. How did Jack feel that night?

Chapter 5: The Giant Beanstalk
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. gigantic - extremely large
	•	2. enormous - very, very big
	•	3. handholds - places where you can grip with your hands
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Why was Jack's room strangely dim when he woke up?
	•	2. What amazing thing had grown in their garden overnight?
	•	3. How big was the beanstalk?
	•	4. What did the beanstalk look like to Jack?
	•	5. What did Jack's mother warn him not to do?

Chapter 6: Climbing to the Clouds
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. curiosity - wanting to know about something
	•	2. sturdy - strong and well-built
	•	3. landscape - the land that you can see around you
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Why did Jack decide to climb the beanstalk?
	•	2. What did the clouds feel like as Jack climbed through them?
	•	3. Where did Jack find himself when he reached the top?
	•	4. What did Jack see in the distance?
	•	5. What did Jack think this place might be?

Chapter 7: The Giant's Castle
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. massive - very large and heavy
	•	2. approached - came closer to
	•	3. rumble - make a low, rolling sound
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How was everything in this land different from Jack's world?
	•	2. Why did Jack become nervous as he approached the castle?
	•	3. What made Jack decide to knock on the door despite his fear?
	•	4. Who opened the door when Jack knocked?
	•	5. How did Jack politely ask for help?

Chapter 8: The Golden Eggs
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. gratefully - with thanks and appreciation
	•	2. suspiciously - with doubt and mistrust
	•	3. commanded - ordered someone to do something
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. Why did the giant's wife tell Jack to eat quickly and hide?
	•	2. What did they hear approaching the castle?
	•	3. Where did Jack hide when the giant came home?
	•	4. What did the giant say when he smelled Jack?
	•	5. What amazing thing did Jack see the golden goose do?

Chapter 9: The Magic Harp
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. amazement - great surprise and wonder
	•	2. soothing - calming and peaceful
	•	3. securely - safely and firmly
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did Jack think about the golden eggs?
	•	2. What did the giant bring out after putting away the goose?
	•	3. What happened when the giant commanded the harp to play?
	•	4. What did Jack take when the giant fell asleep?
	•	5. What happened when Jack touched the magic harp?

Chapter 10: The Giant's Chase
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. bellowed - shouted in a loud, deep voice
	•	2. clutching - holding tightly
	•	3. descent - the act of going down
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did the giant react when he saw Jack with his treasures?
	•	2. What made it difficult for Jack to climb down quickly?
	•	3. What scary thing did Jack see when he looked up?
	•	4. What did Jack shout to his mother?
	•	5. How did Jack's mother help when she heard his calls?

Chapter 11: Chopping Down the Beanstalk
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. directions - different ways or places
	•	2. descend - go down or come down
	•	3. toppling - falling over
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. What did Jack do with the goose and harp when he reached the ground?
	•	2. How hard did Jack work to chop down the beanstalk?
	•	3. What was happening to the giant while Jack was chopping?
	•	4. What happened when Jack made the final swing of the axe?
	•	5. What happened to the giant when the beanstalk fell?

Chapter 12: A New Beginning
Vocabulary Words
	•	1. prosperity - success and wealth
	•	2. handsomely - generously with good payment
	•	3. comfortably - having everything you need
Comprehension Questions
	•	1. How did the golden goose prove it was really magical?
	•	2. How did the magic harp change their home?
	•	3. What did Jack do with the first golden egg?
	•	4. What important lessons did Jack learn from his adventure?
	•	5. What did Jack's mother say about mistakes sometimes becoming good things?

Discussion Questions for the Complete Story
	•	1. Was Jack right to trade the cow for magic beans? Why or why not?
	•	2. What character traits did Jack show during his adventure?
	•	3. How did Jack's lazy habits change by the end of the story?
	•	4. What do you think about Jack taking the giant's treasures?
	•	5. How did Jack show courage when facing the giant?
	•	6. What does this story teach us about taking risks?
	•	7. How did Jack's relationship with his mother change throughout the story?
	•	8. What does the story teach us about the difference between being foolish and being brave?

Writing Prompts
	•	1. Write about a time when you took a risk that turned out well, or when you wish you had been braver.
	•	2. Describe what you think Jack's life was like after the adventure ended.
	•	3. Write the story from the giant's point of view - how did he feel about Jack taking his treasures?
	•	4. Create a new adventure for Jack using one of his magical items.
	•	5. Write about what you would do if you found magic beans and had to decide whether to plant them.
