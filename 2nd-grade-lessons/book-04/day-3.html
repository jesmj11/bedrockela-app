📚 The Magic Stone

The clever traveler walked to the center of the village. He found a nice smooth stone on the ground and picked it up. Then he called out in a loud voice.

"Good people of this village!" he announced. "Since you have no food to share, I will make stone soup for everyone! I have a magic stone that makes delicious soup!"

The villagers peeked out of their windows and doors. A magic stone? Stone soup? They had never heard of such a thing. This sounded very interesting indeed!

The traveler asked for a large pot and some water. One curious villager brought a big pot. Another brought water from the well. Everyone watched as he filled the pot and put it over a fire.

Firefighters

Firefighters are brave people who risk their lives to put out fires and help people during emergencies. When there is a fire, firefighters rush to the scene in fire trucks equipped with hoses, ladders, and other special tools. They wear heavy protective gear to keep themselves safe from heat, smoke, and dangerous chemicals. Firefighters work as a team to rescue people and save buildings from being destroyed by fire. Besides fighting fires, firefighters respond to many other types of emergencies. They help people who are trapped in car accidents, rescue cats stuck in trees, and assist during natural disasters like floods or earthquakes. Many firefighters also teach fire safety to children and adults, showing them how to prevent fires and what to do if a fire starts. Becoming a firefighter requires special training and physical fitness because the job can be very demanding.
