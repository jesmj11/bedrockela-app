📚 A Bold Proposal

After listening to all the reports and complaints about their dangerous situation, a young mouse who was known for his creative thinking stood up and addressed the worried assembly. "I believe I have a solution to our problem," he announced with more confidence than most of the older mice felt was appropriate given their dire circumstances. "We need an early warning system that will tell us when the cat is approaching."

The young mouse continued to explain his ingenious plan. "What we need is to put a bell around the cat's neck. Then, whenever she moves, the bell will ring and warn us of her approach. We'll be able to hear her coming from far away and have plenty of time to hide safely in our burrows. No longer will she be able to sneak up on us and catch us by surprise."

The proposal created immediate excitement throughout the chamber. Mice began chattering enthusiastically about how brilliant this plan was and how it would solve all their problems at once. They could imagine themselves moving freely through the house again, knowing that the ringing bell would always give them advance notice when danger was near. It seemed like the perfect solution to their terrible predicament.

The mice began planning how wonderful their lives would be once the cat wore a warning bell. They could resume their normal activities, venture out to search for food without fear, and enjoy the comfortable existence they had known before this dangerous predator arrived. The more they thought about it, the more convinced they became that this young mouse had found the answer to all their troubles.

📝 Language Lesson

Rhyming Words

Goal: Students will identify and create rhyming words.

Rhyming words sound the SAME at the end. They make poems and stories fun to read aloud!

  • Rhyming Words:
  • cat, hat, mat, sat (all end with -at)
  • play, day, say, way (all end with -ay)
  • book, look, cook, took (all end with -ook)

From today's story: Listen for words that rhyme as you read!

Practice - Find the rhyme: 1. Which word rhymes with "dog"? (log, big, cat) 2. Which word rhymes with "jump"? (run, bump, sit) 3. Which word rhymes with "bed"? (chair, red, sleep)

Remember: Listen to the END of words to find rhymes!
