📚 The Real Wolf Arrives

Several weeks passed quietly on the mountainside, with Marcus continuing his daily routine of shepherding while the villagers went about their business with growing reluctance to respond to any future alarms from the boy who had proven himself unreliable. Then, on one particularly peaceful afternoon when the sheep were grazing contentedly and Marcus was daydreaming in the warm sunshine, a large gray shadow emerged silently from the dense forest that bordered the high pasture.

A genuine wolf, larger and more menacing than any Marcus had ever imagined in his worst nightmares, stepped deliberately into the open meadow with predatory intent clearly visible in his yellow eyes and bared fangs. This was not a figment of imagination or a character in one of his entertaining deceptions, but a real and immediate danger that threatened not only the valuable sheep under his protection, but potentially his own life as well.

Terror gripped Marcus as he watched the fierce predator begin stalking toward the unsuspecting flock with calculated precision and deadly purpose. "Wolf! Wolf!" he screamed with genuine panic and desperate urgency, his voice cracking with authentic fear as he realized the catastrophic situation that was unfolding before his eyes. "Please help me! This time it's real! A wolf is actually attacking the sheep!"

But down in the village, the people who heard his frantic cries simply shook their heads with weary resignation and continued with their daily activities. "There goes Marcus again with another one of his silly pranks," they said to one another dismissively. "We're not going to be fooled a third time by that dishonest boy." They had no way of knowing that this time, his desperate pleas for help were completely genuine, and that both he and the sheep were in mortal danger from a very real predator.
