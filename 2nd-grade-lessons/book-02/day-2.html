📚 Big Engines Say No

The toys called out to the big passenger engine. "Please help us! We need to get over the mountain to the children!" But the big engine shook his head.

"I do not pull toy trains," said the passenger engine. "I only pull important people in fancy cars. I am too important for your job." And away he went.

Soon another engine came down the track. This one was a big freight engine. He was strong and could pull heavy things. Surely he would help the toys!

"Please help us!" called the toys. But the freight engine said, "I am too tired. I have been working all day pulling heavy cars. Find someone else to help you."

📰 Learn Something New!

Helping others

Dentists

Dentists are doctors who take care of people's teeth and gums. They help keep our mouths healthy by cleaning our teeth and checking for problems. Dentists use special tools to look inside our mouths and take X-ray pictures of our teeth. They can see cavities, which are little holes in teeth caused by bacteria. When dentists find cavities, they can fix them by filling the holes with special materials. Dentists also teach people how to brush and floss their teeth properly to prevent cavities. Most people should visit the dentist twice a year for a checkup and cleaning. Going to the dentist regularly helps keep our teeth strong and healthy for our whole lives.
