📚 An Unexpected Solution

Just as Tom was wondering how he could possibly face his mother in such a disheveled state, three unusual visitors appeared in the garden. They were the Puddle-Duck family - Jemima, Rebeccah, and their cousin who lived nearby. The ducks waddled importantly along the path, examining the scattered buttons with great curiosity.

"My goodness!" exclaimed Jemima Puddle-Duck when she noticed Tom's predicament. "What lovely clothes these are! Though they do seem to be rather ill-fitting for a kitten." She picked up one of the brass buttons and admired it carefully. "These would look absolutely splendid on us ducks, don't you think, sisters?"

The three ducks quickly came to an agreement with poor Tom Kitten. They would take his torn clothes and wear them proudly around the farmyard, while Tom could return home in his natural fur coat. "This arrangement will suit everyone perfectly!" declared Rebeccah as they gathered up the jacket, trousers, and remaining buttons.

Within minutes, the three Puddle-Ducks had dressed themselves in Tom's elegant outfit. The clothes fit them much better than they had fit Tom, and they looked quite distinguished waddling about the garden. They thanked Tom politely for his generous contribution to their wardrobe and waddled away proudly, leaving Tom feeling greatly relieved.
