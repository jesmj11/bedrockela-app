📚 A Sweet Discovery

The next morning, Hansel and Gretel woke up cold and hungry. They had slept under a big tree all night. They needed to find food and shelter soon.

As they walked deeper into the woods, they smelled something wonderful. It was sweet and delicious. "Do you smell that?" asked Gretel. "It smells like cookies!"

They followed the sweet smell until they came to a clearing. There, in front of them, stood the most amazing house they had ever seen. It was made entirely of gingerbread and candy!

The walls were made of gingerbread. The roof was covered with frosting. The windows were made of clear sugar candy. Gumdrops decorated the door. It looked good enough to eat!

🤔 Reading Comprehension
