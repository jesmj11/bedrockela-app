📚 Gretel's Clever Plan

For many days, Gretel worked for the witch while Hansel stayed in the cage. Every day the witch checked to see if Hansel was getting fatter. But Hansel was clever too.

When the witch asked Hansel to hold out his finger, he held out a thin chicken bone instead. The witch had poor eyesight and could not tell the difference.

Finally, the witch got tired of waiting. She told Gretel to heat up the big oven. "Fat or thin, I will cook your brother today!" she said with an evil grin.

But when the witch bent over to check the oven, Gretel gave her a big push! The witch fell right into the hot oven. Gretel quickly freed Hansel, and they ran away to safety, finally finding their way home.

🤔 Reading Comprehension

1. How did Hansel trick the witch?

2. How did Gretel defeat the witch?
