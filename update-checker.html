🔄 Force Update Lessons

⚠️ Are you seeing old content or blue screens?
This tool will force your browser to download fresh lesson files.

What This Does:

  1. Unregisters all service workers
  2. Deletes ALL cached lesson files
  3. Clears browser storage
  4. Forces fresh downloads from server
  5. Reloads the page

Manual Method (if button doesn't work):

  1. Chrome/Edge: Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
  2. Firefox: Press Ctrl+F5 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
  3. Safari: Hold Shift and click the reload button
